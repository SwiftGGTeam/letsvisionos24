FAQ

FAQ

0. Is there a live steaming of the event?

No, this event is an offline event and there will not be a live streaming.

1. Will lunch and dinner be provided on the day of the event?

Yes. Our event will provide lunch and dinner for all attendees, mainly consisting of Western cuisine such as pizza, burgers, and sandwiches, as well as a variety of snacks such as roasted chicken wings, vegetable salad, and of course, drinks such as cola, Sprite, and mineral water.

  1. What is the use of the stamp collection card?

The stamp collection card can be used for a lottery. Users who purchase general admission tickets and VIP tickets will receive a stamp collection card, which can be used to participate in the interactive games of exhibitors in the exhibition area. When you have collected a certain number of stamps, you can participate in a lottery at the organizer's booth.

3. Are there any recommended hotels and dining services around the conference center?

We have prepared a guide for all attendees who have purchased tickets, including accommodation, travel, and other aspects. The online version of the guide can be accessed by clicking -> 📖. We hope this event will leave you with a beautiful memory.

4. How should non-Chinese citizens purchase tickets?

The ticketing platform "Eventbrite" supports international payment methods such as PayPal, Visa/Mastercard, etc. You can refer to our guide for purchasing tickets -> 📖. If you have any problems purchasing tickets, please feel free to contact us.

5. What is Zolplay?

Zolplay is a software development company with a super hero spirit and is also a trendy toy brand for programmers. This conference has a deep cooperation with Zuo Wan, bringing a highly trendy gift for all attendees.

6. Is there a group discount for tickets?

Yes, when purchasing tickets in groups of 3 or more (including 3), you can enjoy a 10% discount.

7. What's in the VIP special gift box?

In addition to the carefully designed VIP gift box itself, it contains a limited edition Let's visionOS 2024 conference memorabilia and a Let's visionOS x Zuo Wan collaborative trendy jacket.

8. What is the welcome dinner in the VIP ticket privilege?

On March 29, 2024, the organizer will invite speakers, some sponsors, and VIP ticket holders to participate in a private welcome dinner. Here, you can have one-on-one in-depth exchanges with others. The welcome dinner is expected to begin at 6:00 p.m. on March 29, 2024. Please reserve your time in advance for those who have purchased VIP tickets.

9. The Chinese speech is a little challenging for me. What should I do if I don't understand it?

Please be assured that language differences are fully taken into account. In order to ensure that each participant can fully understand the shared content, we have specially prepared English transcripts that match the theme. At the event, you can use these materials to help understand the sharing of speakers. Our goal is to make the learning experience accessible to everyone involved.
